## Persona A — Yoni, the Oleh Chadash

### Background
- **Age / status:** 23M, made aliyah ~6 months ago from Russia/Ukraine
- **Hebrew:** conversational but slow reader. Does NOT know driving vocabulary:
  - תמרור (road sign), רכב כבד (heavy vehicle), נתיב (lane), צומת מרומזר (signalized intersection), עקיפה (overtaking), האטה (deceleration), מתן זכות קדימה (yielding right of way), מנורה (traffic light), מדרכה (sidewalk)
- **Motivation:** LOW. Parents nag him; he'd rather play games. Will abandon in < 5 min if it feels punishing, confusing, or too "school-like."
- **Tech comfort:** high (smartphone native, used to fast UX).
- **Session pattern:** 10-20 min, irregular. Opens app, does a few questions, closes it.
- **Device:** phone (mobile-first assumption).

### Journey script (ordered tasks for Phase 2 walkthrough)

| Step | Task | Expected path | What Yoni thinks if it works | What breaks him |
|------|------|--------------|------------------------------|-----------------|
| J1 | Open `/` cold (first ever visit) | See hero, read the H1 | "OK, זה לתיאוריה, אני מבין" | Doesn't understand what the app IS in 10 sec |
| J2 | Find where to start | Click the primary CTA (blue button "בוא נתחיל בתמרורים?") | Starts on something visual (signs — good for vocab learner) | CTA text is unclear; no English/icon hint |
| J3 | Answer 5 sign questions on `/study/תמרורים` | Click each, read question, pick answer | Gets feedback after each answer | Hits a word he can't read; no tooltip/glossary; multiple-choice but no image aid |
| J4 | Encounter an unknown Hebrew word mid-question | Read question with "נתיב" or "עקיפה" | Ideally: can tap/hover word for definition | Only option: open Google Translate in another tab = UX death |
| J5 | Get one answer wrong intentionally | Pick wrong answer | Sees explanation that teaches WHY | Feedback is just "שגוי" with no explanation |
| J6 | Try the daily challenge (`/daily`) | Navigate from home or nav | Short, achievable — feels like a win | Can't find it; or it's too hard; or no encouragement text |
| J7 | Open `/progress` after 5 questions | Navigate to progress | Understands he's seen some questions, has a rough % | Empty state OR confusing metrics he can't interpret |
| J8 | Close the browser, reopen app next day | Revisit `/` | App shows "continue from where you left off" | Nothing personalized; feels like starting over |

### Friction hypotheses (to verify in Phase 2)
- **F-A1 (HIGH):** No glossary — technical terms appear inline with no explanation. Kills comprehension for new immigrants.
- **F-A2 (HIGH):** Question feedback shows "נכון/שגוי" but explanation quality varies. Low-scoring explanations feel punishing.
- **F-A3 (MED):** Homepage has no clear "easiest start" signal. "בוא נתחיל בתמרורים?" is the CTA but תמרורים (signs) is not obviously easier than חוקי התנועה (traffic laws) to Yoni.
- **F-A4 (MED):** No warm re-entry on returning visit — ContinueTile helps but is below the fold; Yoni may not scroll.
- **F-A5 (LOW):** Language is 100% Hebrew with no English fallback. Expected for this app, but worth noting.

### Pass/fail criteria
- ✅ PASS: Completes 10 questions without external tools. Doesn't quit. Returns day 2.
- ⚠️ PARTIAL: Completes questions but had to Google one term. Acceptable if rare.
- ❌ FAIL: Quits within first 5 min. OR says "I don't know what this word means and there's nowhere to find out."

## Persona B — Maya, the Israeli Sophomore

### Background
- **Age / status:** 17F, native Israeli, 11th grade (שכבה י"א), wants to drive parents' car
- **Hebrew:** native, including driving vocabulary — no language barrier at all
- **Motivation:** VERY HIGH. Has a test date in ~3 weeks. Will grind daily. Treats this like exam prep, not casual learning.
- **Tech comfort:** very high.
- **Session pattern:** 1-2 hr focused blocks. Will use every feature. Wants efficiency.
- **Device:** laptop + phone (both).

### Journey script (ordered tasks for Phase 3 walkthrough)

| Step | Task | Expected path | What Maya thinks if it works | What breaks her |
|------|------|--------------|------------------------------|-----------------|
| J1 | Open `/` — how fast to mock exam? | See CTA, navigate to `/exam` | Reaches exam config in < 5 clicks from homepage | 3+ intermediate pages with no "go straight to exam" shortcut |
| J2 | Configure + take full 30-question exam | Set length=30, topic=all, start | Smooth flow; timer-like pressure | Exam config is missing; no "full standard exam" preset |
| J3 | View result at `/results/[id]` | Auto-redirect after submit | Sees: score, %, pass/fail, question-by-question breakdown | Result is just a number; no drill-down; no context on what she missed |
| J4 | From results: drill her mistakes immediately | Click "תרגל שאלות שטעית" or equivalent | One-click → exam retake or `/mistakes` with those specific questions | Has to manually navigate to `/mistakes`; friction kills momentum |
| J5 | Use `/review` smart session | Navigate to חזרה חכמה | 20 questions weighted toward her weak spots | Empty state because she's new; or the weighting isn't visible to her |
| J6 | Check exam readiness on `/progress` | Navigate to progress | Sees per-topic accuracy %, understands "I'm 72% ready for בטיחות" | No readiness signal; just raw counts that don't map to "will I pass?" |
| J7 | Bookmark a hard question, find it in `/saved` | Star icon → `/saved` | Instant; works on mobile too | Bookmark icon isn't obvious; or saved page doesn't link back to study mode |
| J8 | Take 2nd exam after drilling | Repeat J2 | Sees improvement in score / progress | No delta shown; feels like starting from scratch each time |

### Friction hypotheses (to verify in Phase 3)
- **F-B1 (HIGH):** No "exam readiness %" on progress page — Maya wants a single number. She gets per-topic accuracy but no synthesized score.
- **F-B2 (HIGH):** From results, drilling mistakes requires manual navigation. One-click "retake weak questions" flow is missing or buried.
- **F-B3 (MED):** Homepage CTA pushes to `/study/תמרורים` (topic study), not `/exam` (mock test). Maya would prefer direct access to the exam.
- **F-B4 (MED):** Exam config screen — no "standard official exam" preset (30 questions, random, all topics). Every visit requires re-configuring.
- **F-B5 (LOW):** No delta tracking — after 5 exams, Maya can't see "I went from 65% → 82% over the week."

### Pass/fail criteria
- ✅ PASS: Takes mock exam within 60 sec of opening. Sees score. Knows what to drill next from the result page. Finds progress that tells her "ready vs. not ready."
- ⚠️ PARTIAL: Gets to exam quickly but can't one-click drill mistakes. Acceptable if fix is low-effort.
- ❌ FAIL: Spends > 2 min finding the mock exam. OR progress page gives her numbers but no actionable signal.

After reviewing the app: the `/mistakes` and `/review` routes already serve this persona, and his journey script would largely overlap with Maya's Phase 3 walk. **Skipping Persona C** — the two existing personas cover the UX surface.
